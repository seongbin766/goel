About

G.O.E.L Computer Educational Initiative utilizes a Trifunctional system designed to cultivate critical thinking and problem-solving skills in order to elevate computer literacy among the youth of Zvishavane. Our customers have access to critical mind-challenging [individual and teamwork] computer gaming; and a fast browsing WIFI network. More so a computer literacy oriented service facility: dedicated to teaching youth in communities on computer operation packages, the basic functions and uses of a computer in everyday life in the initial year of establishment.

Mission Statement

To cultivate critical thinking - problem-solving skills and elevate computer literacy among the youth of Zvishavane through computer gaming.

Vision

To be the leading computer operations, packages and programs educational facility in Zimbabwe.

Message by Managing Partner

As a for purpose organization, dedicated to the upliftment of sustainable development: G.O.E.L seeks to rewrite the narrative on the life of most Youth around the Zvishavane community by empowering the youth within the local-urban and rural community with ICT Skill sets. This we strongly believe will empower them to take advantage of opportunities around and across the globe.
